quizFrequently Asked Questions

QHow do I upload a PDF question bank?

Navigate to the Dashboard and click on "PDF to Mock Test". Upload your file (max 10MB) and ensure it follows the numbered format (1., 2., etc.). The system will auto-extract questions.

QCan I customize the test duration?

Yes. In the test configuration step, you can manually input the duration in minutes. The default is set to 60 minutes for standard exams.

QWhat happens if my internet disconnects during a test?

TestArena saves progress locally every 30 seconds. Once your connection is restored, the data will sync automatically. Do not close the browser tab.

QHow is negative marking calculated?

If enabled, 0.25 marks are deducted for every incorrect answer. Unattempted questions do not attract any penalty.
