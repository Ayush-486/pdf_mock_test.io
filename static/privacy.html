1. Data Collection

When you register for and use TestArena, we collect personal information including but not limited to your full name, email address, institutional affiliation, and examination history.

We also automatically collect technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, and operating system details to ensure the integrity and security of the Computer-Based Testing environment.

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, username or similar identifier.
  • Contact Data: Billing address, delivery address, email address and telephone numbers.
  • Technical Data: Internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version.

Your data is primarily used to administer exams, process results, and generate performance reports. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
